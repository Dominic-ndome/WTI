About

Watan Training Institute is a premier tertiary institution located in Easleigh, NAIROBI. We are mandated to operate as an Education and Training Institution by The Technical and Vocational Education and Training Authority (TVETA)!

A College Dedicated To Your Success

Preparing You for the Future with In-Demand Technical Skills

We have ideal programs for anyone who has difficulties in attending College regularly, perhaps through employment commitments, shift working, a young family, travelling distances, and disability or family responsibility.

None of these need prevent anyone from gaining qualifications.

A College That Puts Students First in exceptional Learning Environments

Watan Training Institute has a first rate academic program, a fully qualified teaching staff, bright and comfortable classrooms, continuous student counseling and advising department, and a very effective and enthusiastic student support staff.

Mission

M

We aspire to foster high quality academic and career success through proper training, development of critical thinking and effective communication that will create and manage innovative value adding solutions to our clients and those served by them.Our goal is to empower individuals with the practical skills and knowledge necessary to lead in today’s evolving industries.

Vision

V

We envisions ourself to be an excellent technology, education and technical skills training center in shaping the future of the world.We aim to be a leader in innovation, providing cutting-edge programs that adapt to the ever-changing demands of the global workforce. Our commitment is to inspire and equip individuals to become the pioneers and problem-solvers of tomorrow.

History

H

The institute was established in January 2022 as a Technical Training Institute focused to dispense knowledge and skill-based training to the surrounding community members. As a middle level technical college in the modern Kenyan society, the college was crafted to provide technical training for the middle level man power for both the private and public sectors of the economy.
